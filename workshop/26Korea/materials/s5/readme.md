# 🟪 Session 5: AI Storytelling & Storyboard Development

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, participants will be able to:

- Understand the importance of storytelling in innovation and product promotion.
- Develop a compelling narrative for an AI project.
- Write effective scene descriptions using ChatGPT.
- Create a professional video script.
- Plan camera shots for a promotional video.
- Develop a complete storyboard for a hackathon presentation.

# 📖 Why Storytelling Matters

People rarely remember facts alone.

They remember stories.

A great story helps people:

- Understand your idea
- Connect emotionally
- Remember your project
- Believe in your solution

Every successful product tells a story.

Examples include:

- Apple
- Tesla
- Airbnb
- Grab
- Netflix

They do not simply describe features.

They explain:

- What problem exists
- Why it matters
- How their solution changes people's lives

# 🎬 What is Storytelling?

Storytelling is the process of communicating ideas through a sequence of meaningful events.

Instead of saying:

> Our application uses AI to improve parking.

Tell a story.

> Every morning, students spend twenty minutes searching for parking spaces before class. Our AI solution helps them find available parking instantly, saving time and reducing stress.

The second version is much more engaging.

# 🎯 Storytelling Structure

A simple storytelling framework is:

```
Problem

↓

Challenge

↓

Solution

↓

Benefits

↓

Future Vision
```

Every hackathon project should follow this structure.

# 💡 The Hero's Journey for Innovation

Your users are the heroes.

Your AI project is the solution that helps them overcome a challenge.

Example

Student

↓

Cannot find parking

↓

Late for class

↓

Uses SmartPark AI

↓

Finds parking instantly

↓

Arrives on time

↓

Better campus experience

# 📝 Building Your Story

Every project story should answer these questions.

| Question | Example |
|----------|----------|
| Who is the user? | University student |
| What problem do they face? | Difficulty finding parking |
| Why is the problem important? | Wastes time and causes stress |
| What is the AI solution? | SmartPark AI |
| How does it improve life? | Saves time and reduces congestion |

# 🎥 Storytelling for Promotional Videos

A promotional video should not explain every technical detail.

Instead, it should answer:

- What is the problem?
- Why should people care?
- What is the solution?
- Why is it better?
- Why should people support it?

Keep the message simple.

# 🎬 What is a Storyboard?

A storyboard is a visual plan for a video.

It shows:

- Scene sequence
- Camera shots
- Narration
- Dialogue
- Visual elements

Think of it as a comic strip for your video.

# 🎞️ Typical Storyboard Structure

| Scene | Visual | Narration | Duration |
|--------|---------|-----------|----------|
| 1 | Student searching for parking | Every morning... | 8 sec |
| 2 | Parking full | Students waste valuable time. | 8 sec |
| 3 | SmartPark AI App | Meet SmartPark AI. | 10 sec |
| 4 | Student follows directions | AI finds the nearest parking space. | 12 sec |
| 5 | Happy student | Save time. Reduce stress. | 8 sec |

# 🎥 Camera Shot Types

Different camera angles create different emotions.

| Shot | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Wide Shot | Show the environment |
| Medium Shot | Show actions |
| Close-up | Show emotions or important details |
| Over-the-Shoulder | Show interaction with technology |
| Aerial Shot | Show campus or city overview |

Use a variety of shots to make your video more engaging.

# 🎙️ Writing Narration

Good narration is:

- Short
- Clear
- Natural
- Conversational

Avoid reading long paragraphs.

Example

❌ Poor

> SmartPark AI is an intelligent parking management system developed using advanced artificial intelligence algorithms...

✅ Better

> Finding parking shouldn't be stressful. SmartPark AI helps students find available parking in seconds.

# ✍️ Writing a Video Script with ChatGPT

Example Prompt

```
You are a professional video script writer.

Write a one-minute promotional video script for an AI-powered Smart Campus Parking System.

The script should include narration, scene descriptions, and camera shot suggestions.

Use simple English.

The audience is university students.
```

# 🎨 Generating Scene Descriptions

ChatGPT can describe every scene in detail.

Example Prompt

```
Describe Scene 3 for a promotional video.

The scene shows a university student using an AI-powered parking application.

Include the background, camera angle, lighting, emotions, and visual style.
```

# 🧩 Exercise 1 — Develop Your Story

## Objective

Transform your project idea into a compelling story.

Discuss as a team:

- Who is your main user?
- What problem does the user face?
- Why is it important?
- How does your AI solution solve the problem?
- What is the final outcome?

Write your story in **150 words or fewer**.

## Objective

Prompt Example

```
Write a one-minute promotional video script for our AI hackathon project.

The audience is university students.

Include narration and scene descriptions.
```

Review and improve the script as a team.

# 💡 Tips for Great Storytelling

Always remember:

✅ Focus on the user.

✅ Start with a real problem.

✅ Keep the story simple.

✅ Show the impact.

✅ Use emotions.

✅ End with a strong message.

People remember stories far longer than technical specifications.
