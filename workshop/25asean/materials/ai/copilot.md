## 1. Introduction

[Microsoft Copilot](https://copilot.microsoft.com/) is an AI-powered productivity assistant integrated into Microsoft 365 applications. It enhances creativity, productivity, and collaboration by leveraging large language models (LLMs) and Microsoft Graph. Copilot provides real-time support across apps like Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Teams, making it a valuable tool for daily operations and statistical work in the Department of Statistics Malaysia.

### Key Features:

- **Content Generation:** Drafts reports, emails, and presentations based on user prompts.
- **Data Analysis:** Assists in Excel with formatting, graph creation, pivot tables, trend identification, and summarization.
- **Meeting Summaries:** Summarizes discussions and key actions from meetings in Microsoft Teams.

## 2. How to Use Microsoft Copilot: A Step-by-Step Guide

*Case Study: Preparing Monthly Labour Force Statistics Report*

### **Step 1: Access Microsoft Copilot**

- **Within Microsoft Word or Excel:**
  - Open a new or existing document or spreadsheet.
  - Click on the **Copilot** icon in the toolbar to activate the assistant.

### **Step 2: Define the Objective**

- In the Copilot chat interface, input the objective:
  > *"Generate a summary report of Malaysia's Labour Force Statistics for October 2025 based on uploaded Excel data."*

### **Step 3: Generate Initial Content**

- Copilot will analyze the data and draft:
  - **Executive Summary:** Key highlights and trends.
  - **Charts and Tables:** Automatically generated visuals.
  - **Narrative Analysis:** Interpretation of employment, unemployment, and participation rates.

### **Step 4: Refine and Expand Content**

- Provide specific prompts to elaborate:
  > *"Add comparison with September 2025 data and highlight significant changes."*

- Copilot will update the report accordingly.

### **Step 5: Collaborate and Iterate**

- Share the document via **Microsoft Teams** or **OneDrive**.
- Collaborate with team members to review and enhance the report.

### **Step 6: Finalize the Document**

- Use Copilot to proofread, format, and generate a summary.
- Export the report to PDF or presentation format.
