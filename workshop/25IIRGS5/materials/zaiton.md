# Succeeding in Your Viva ‚Äî What Examiners Expect & How to Show Your Best Work

 <img src="https://github.com/drshahizan/short-course/blob/main/workshop/25IIRGS5/images/iirg%20zaiton.jpeg" alt="Shahizan SLR"  height="300">
 
In this insightful session, **Prof. Dr. Siti Zaiton Mohd Hashim** presents a practical and empowering guide to succeeding in a PhD viva, demystifying what examiners truly expect and how candidates can confidently demonstrate mastery of their research. The presentation reframes the viva as a scholarly conversation‚Äîone that assesses originality, rigour, and contribution‚Äîwhile highlighting the qualities examiners value most, such as clarity, critical thinking, methodological consistency, and honest awareness of limitations.

Designed to reduce anxiety and strengthen candidates‚Äô readiness, this session offers actionable strategies for preparation, including developing a clear mental map of the thesis, articulating contributions succinctly, and delivering structured, coherent responses. It also warns against common pitfalls that could undermine performance. With its motivating tone and research-backed advice, this talk is essential for any postgraduate student aiming to enter the viva room with confidence, clarity, and academic maturity.


# **Key Points**

* **Understand the True Purpose of the Viva**
  Learn why the viva is not an interrogation but a scholarly dialogue.

* **Know What Examiners Look For**
  Master the core criteria: originality, rigour, contribution, critical thinking, methodological consistency, and limitation awareness.

* **Articulate Your Thesis Contribution Clearly**
  Identify and express your main contribution‚Äîmodel, framework, method, dataset, or theory‚Äîin just a few sentences.

* **Avoid Common Pitfalls**
  Prevent mistakes such as defensiveness, overly long answers, overreliance on slides, poor justification of decisions, and getting lost in technical details.

* **Prepare with Strategy, Not Memorisation**
  Build a mental map of your thesis, revisit your research questions, understand your methodology deeply, identify key papers, and practise your thesis summary.

* **Strengthen Your Research Culture**
  Embrace integrity, curiosity, openness to critique, and patience throughout the research journey.

* **Show Confidence the Right Way**
  Communicate with structured reasoning, calmness, and ownership of your scholarly work.

* **Walk Into the Viva Knowing Examiners Want You to Pass**
  The session reinforces that you have already done the work‚Äîthe viva is your opportunity to tell your research story with clarity and pride.
