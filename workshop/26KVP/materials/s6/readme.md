# 🎥 What Makes a Good Promotional Video?

A good promotional video should:

- Capture attention immediately
- Explain the problem clearly
- Present the solution simply
- Highlight the benefits
- End with a memorable message

Remember:

People decide within the first few seconds whether they will continue watching.

# 🎯 Recommended Video Structure

Your promotional video should follow this sequence.

| Scene | Content | Duration |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Opening | Introduce the problem | 10 sec |
| Challenge | Show why the problem matters | 15 sec |
| Solution | Introduce your AI project | 20 sec |
| Features | Demonstrate key functions | 20 sec |
| Benefits | Explain the impact | 10 sec |
| Closing | Project logo and slogan | 5 sec |

Total duration:

**1–2 minutes**

# 🖼️ Preparing Visual Assets

Your visuals may include:

- AI-generated images
- Project poster
- Icons
- Illustrations
- Screenshots
- Mobile application mock-ups
- Team photographs
- Short video clips

Ensure that all visuals are:

- High quality
- Relevant
- Consistent in style

# 🎙️ AI Narration

Narration should be:

- Clear
- Natural
- Confident
- Easy to understand

Keep each sentence short.

Example

❌ Poor

> Our project utilises advanced artificial intelligence technology integrated with multiple intelligent computational algorithms...

✅ Better

> SmartPark AI helps students find parking quickly, saving time and reducing stress.

# 🎵 Background Music

Choose music that:

- Matches the mood
- Is not too loud
- Does not distract from narration

Suggested styles:

- Inspirational
- Modern
- Technology
- Corporate
- Upbeat

Always ensure you have permission to use music or select royalty-free audio.

# 🎞️ Video Editing

Recommended tools include:

- Canva
- CapCut
- Adobe Express
- Microsoft Clipchamp
- DaVinci Resolve
- iMovie

Editing checklist:

- Smooth transitions
- Consistent fonts
- Readable text
- Appropriate pacing
- Clear narration
- Balanced audio

# 🤖 AI Tools for Video Production

Generative AI can assist with:

- Script writing
- Voice generation
- Subtitle creation
- Image generation
- Background removal
- Video enhancement
- Music suggestions
- Caption generation

Remember:

AI assists the creative process.

Your team makes the final decisions.

# 📊 Team Collaboration

Successful hackathon teams divide responsibilities.

Example

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Team Leader | Coordinate project |
| Prompt Engineer | Generate AI prompts |
| Content Writer | Prepare text |
| Designer | Create poster and visuals |
| Video Editor | Produce final video |
| Presenter | Deliver presentation |

Everyone should contribute.

# 📢 Preparing Your Final Presentation

Your presentation should answer five questions.

1. What problem are you solving?

2. Why is the problem important?

3. What is your AI solution?

4. What makes your solution unique?

5. What impact could your project create?

Keep your explanation simple and confident.

# 🧩 Exercise 1 — Produce Your Promotional Video

## Objective

Produce a **1–2 minute promotional video** for your AI hackathon project.

Your video should include:

- Project title
- AI-generated visuals
- Problem statement
- Proposed solution
- Key features
- Benefits
- Closing slogan

# 🧩 Exercise 2 — Add AI Narration

## Objective

Create narration for your promotional video.

Use ChatGPT to improve your script.

Your narration should:

- Explain the problem
- Introduce the solution
- Highlight the benefits
- End with a memorable message

Keep the narration between **150–200 words**.

# 🧩 Exercise 4 — Hackathon Presentation

Each team delivers a **5-minute presentation**.

Suggested presentation structure:
