# ‚ùì FAQs regarding the **Open Access (OA) agreement between Malaysia and Springer Nature

## 1. **Who is considered the corresponding author?**

The corresponding author is the individual responsible for managing the manuscript and all correspondence throughout the publication process‚Äîfrom submission and peer review to revisions and final approval. This author acts on behalf of all co-authors and serves as the primary contact for any post-publication inquiries. 

## 2. **How can I ensure my article is eligible under the agreement?**

To confirm eligibility:

* Submit your manuscript to a journal covered by the agreement.
* Use at least one of the recognized methods to identify your institutional affiliation during submission.
* Upon acceptance, provide accurate institutional details when prompted.

## 3. **Which article types are covered by this agreement?**

The agreement covers the following article types:

* **Original Paper**: Standard articles presenting new research findings.
* **Review Paper**: Articles summarizing and interpreting existing research.

## 4. **What if my institution isn't part of the agreement?**

If your institution isn't included, you can explore alternative funding options for Article Processing Charges (APCs). Visit Springer Nature's open access funding page to check for available resources.

## 5. **Who can I contact for more information?**

For further assistance or inquiries about the agreement, please email: [oa.verification@springernature.com](mailto:oa.verification@springernature.com).
